# Project Brief

## 1. Goal
- What is the business goal?
- What result defines success?

## 2. Audience
- Who is the main audience?
- What are their pains and expectations?

## 3. Scope
- Pages / modules
- Required integrations
- Required dashboards / flows

## 4. Visual direction
- Brand or company
- Approved palette
- References
- Typography preferences
- Motion preference

## 5. Technical direction
- Preferred stack
- Hosting / deployment
- Database / auth
- Analytics / monitoring

## 6. Delivery constraints
- Deadline
- MVP vs full version
- Risks
