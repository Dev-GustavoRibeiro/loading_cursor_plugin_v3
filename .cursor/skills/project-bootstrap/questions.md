# Discovery Questions

1. What is the project and what business result should it achieve?
2. Who is the primary audience?
3. What pages, modules, or flows must exist?
4. Is there an existing brand or should Loading defaults be used?
5. What is the approved palette in hex?
6. Are there visual references or a Figma link?
7. Which stack is preferred?
8. Which integrations are required?
9. Is this an MVP, prototype, or production build?
10. What is the timeline?
